About

I'm a Master's student at Bocconi University working on my thesis on urban mobility in New York City. My research integrates multi-modal transport data — yellow cab, rideshare (HVFHV), Citi Bike, and MTA subway — to model how people move across the city.

I'm interested in the intersection of data engineering, spatial analysis, and urban economics.
